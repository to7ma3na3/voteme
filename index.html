VOTING FLOW

Vote Me Voting System WEBSITE

  1. Open Vote Me WEBSITE and make account with your voter registration detailes and password.
  2. Access to vote registration page with your 8 digits voter-number and check your vote record. At first, there's none.
  3. you can register your ballot also change the registration, if it is before the summarization date.
  4. After the date, aggregation computer sends you the result of the election and also your final vote.

Vote Me aggregation COMPUTER

  1. Vote Me COMPUTER reads and saves the voter list of the county up to 1 million. It also makes all voters' address-form for mailing.
  2. It makes enough voter-numbers for voters. Each voter-number has different random 8 digits number to identify voter safely.
  3. It gives each voter-number different sets of randam 3 digits number. Each of those represents a candidate.
  4. It makes the ballot-forms for mailing, which contains set of voter-number and candidate-numbers.
  5. It blocks unlisted voter's making account or registering ballot with wrong voter's number.
  6. It saves all voters' ballots from the website, from mails at the election committee and from pollimg places.
  7. It reads 8 digits and 3 digits number on each ballot and answers candidate's official index number.
  8. It summeraizes the data and shows the result. Turnout, total number of votes, valid and invalid votes, number of votes per candidate, etc.
  9. It sends the results of the summarization and own vote to voters.
  10. It can be deleted afer the results got approved.
